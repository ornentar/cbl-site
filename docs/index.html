CARBON BASED LIFEFORMS

Johannes Hedberg  &  Daniel Segerstad

"Combining sounds of earth and space in fine-tuned, but still solid, musical visions, seldom forgetting the irreplaceable TB-303."

The CBL Story

Swedish ambient duo Carbon Based Lifeforms was founded in Gothenburg in 1996 by Daniel Ringström & Johannes Hedberg. 1998 album The Path (initially released as a Notch recording) combined atmospheric synthesizers, downtempo beats, acid basslines from a Roland TB-303, didgeridoo, and field recordings. The album was released under the CBL moniker in 1999. The duo signed with French ambient/trance label Ultimae Records in 2002, and released Hydroponic Garden in 2003. The album's downtempo acid ambient style proved to be a cult favorite, and follow-up World of Sleepers arrived in 2006. In November of 2009, CBL played two sold-out concerts at IMAX dome/planetarium Cosmonova inside Stockholm's Museum of Natural History. After scrapping sessions for a third album in 2009, the less acid-tinged Interloper finally appeared in 2010, at which point Ringström was known as Daniel Segerstad. CBL switched to beatless ambient music in 2011 with the release of fourth album TwentyThree, as well as the hour-long drone piece VLA. In 2015, the duo signed with Blood Music and began remastering some of their older albums for release on vinyl. In October of 2017, they released the long-awaited fifth full effort, Derelicts.

Musical Motifs

Everyday Sounds

CBL incorporates everyday sounds into their music in a meaningful but often obscured way. You may hear dogs barking car horns, or other everyday sounds skillfully layered into the tracks.

Technology & Science

Showing a clear influence from science fiction, industry, and electronics, you'll notice noises and samples that tell a story of humankind's relationship with technology.

Nature

The duo includes a variety of natural sounds taken from field recordings that they have collected, and weaves them masterfully into their sound.

Ethereal Ambience

Known first and foremost as ambient musicians, in all of their music you will find a certain airy, dreamlike quality that transports you to another place.
